The Birth of EPL

After the success of Football league First Division, which is a competition founded from 1888, the clubs participated in this league have come away the Football league organization and start to organize a new league called English Premier league or EPL in 1992. The aim of EPL is to find the strongest clubs in EPL is to find out the strongest English club of a season and maximize the profit from broadcasting and sponsorship contracts because they can choose the TV company to broadcasting. ,

The EPL officially established on 20 February 1992 and Sky TV is the company who win the bidding of TV rights of EPL even the idea of separated from Football league come from Greg Dyke, director of LWT (London Weekend Television). The EPL first campaign started on 15 August 1922 with 22 clubs but at the end of the 1994/95 season the intention to reduce the number of clubs to 20 have been accepted when four clubs were relegated and there is only two promoted.

The first match of EPL is between Manchester United and Sheffield United with the result of 2-1 when Sheffield United get 3 points after first 90 minutes of EPL. The first champion of EPL is Manchester United, they achieved the title with 10 points lead over runner-up Aston Villa with the total of 84 pts.
